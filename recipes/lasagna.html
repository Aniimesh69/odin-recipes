Classic Lasagna

Ingredients:

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the Bechamel Sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups milk, warmed
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

For the Lasagna:

  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package directions
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Nutrition Facts per Serving:

Calories: 500

Fat: 20g

Carbohydrates: 50g

Protein: 25g

Steps to Cook:

Make the Meat Sauce:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Add ground beef and cook, breaking up the meat with a wooden spoon, until browned.
  3. Add onion and garlic and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Make the Bechamel Sauce:

  1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Whisk in flour and cook, whisking constantly, for 1 minute.
  3. Slowly whisk in warmed milk, continuing to whisk until the sauce is smooth and thick, about 5 minutes.
  4. Whisk in nutmeg, salt, and pepper.

Assemble the Lasagna:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  3. Top with a layer of lasagna noodles, then spread with a layer of ricotta cheese. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  4. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce, then top with mozzarella cheese.
  5. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Uncover the baking dish and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.

Enjoy your delicious homemade Lasagna!
