Decadent Brownies

These rich and fudgy brownies are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Easy to make and perfect for sharing!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder, sifted
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips (optional)

Nutrition Facts:

Per brownie (based on 12 servings):

  • Calories: 320
  • Fat: 19g
  • Saturated fat: 11g
  • Cholesterol: 55mg
  • Sodium: 180mg
  • Carbohydrates: 37g
  • Fiber: 2g
  • Sugar: 29g
  • Protein: 3g

Steps to Cook:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together sugar, flour, and cocoa powder.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together melted butter, eggs, vanilla extract, and salt.
  4. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips, if desired.
  6. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs.
  7. Let cool completely before cutting and serving.

Enjoy your delicious homemade brownies!
